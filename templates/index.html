The CoViD-19 pandemic has brought tremendous destruction to the world. It has resulted in huge numbers of deaths and the consequences include economic collapse and widespread loss of jobs. It is neccessary to follow personal safety precautions and curb this pandemic before it causes any more destruction.


How to protect yourselves and others around you


Do not sneeze or cough without covering
Follow social distancing and avoid close contact with others

Wash your hands for atleast 20 secs with hand-wash frequently
Do not touch any parts of your face, to avoid contamination

Wear a mask always when outdoors
Stay indoors if you exhibit crucial symptoms
